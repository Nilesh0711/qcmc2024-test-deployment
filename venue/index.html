Venue of QCMC 2022

QCMC 2022, the fifteenth edition of the International Conference on Quantum Communication, Measurement and Computing (QCMC), will take place at the Calouste Gulbenkian Foundation, in Lisbon.

Address : Avenida de Berna, 45A, Lisbon, Portugal

How to get there : Directions and public transport information

Map:

Rooms:

To reach the QCMC 2022 area, enter the main building of the Calouste Gulbenkian Foundation , go left, and take the stairs down.

And the end of the stairs, you will find:

  • The Registration Desk, straight ahead.
  • The QCMC conference room Auditório 2, on your left.
  • The poster session room Sala 2, straight ahead, past the Registration Desk area, at the end of the corridor.
  • The coffee break/lunch area, on your left.
  • Restrooms, on your right
  • The poster session room Sala do Foyer above the coffee break/lunch area, with a dedicated stairway.

Please note the venue closes at 18:00, but the gardens remain open until sunset.
