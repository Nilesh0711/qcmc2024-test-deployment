Registration Desk

The Registration Desk opens on Monday, at 9:45. Please check-in early, and collect your badge there.

To reach the Registration Desk, enter the main building of the Calouste Gulbenkian Foundation , go left, and take the stairs down. At the end of the stairs, walk straigh ahead to the registration area.

See the venue page for more details about the conference and poster rooms.

All lunches and coffee breaks, as well as the conference dinner, are included in the registration (see details in the corresponding section below).

Wi-Fi

QCMC will have two dedicated Wi-Fi networks at Fundação Calouste Gulbenkian (FCG):

  • FCG Eventos
  • FCG Eventos 5 Ghz

You can choose any of them. The corresponding password (the same for both networks) will be available locally.

The open FCG Wi-Fi is available to anyone visiting Fundação Calouste Gulbenkian (FCG), but may be slower.

Posters

Please find the session and room for your poster in the Programme Booklet.

There is one board per poster. You can freely choose in which board to put your poster. There will be double-sided tape available in the room to attach your poster to the board.

The rooms for the poster sessions will open at the start of the conference each day:

  • Please put your poster in the board of your choice at any time during the day, ahead of the poster session, i.e. at the latest during the afternoon coffee break.
  • Please remove your poster at the end of the session. The rooms will close at 18:00.

The maximum size for the posters is A0.

Shops (unaffiliated to QCMC) providing local printing for posters include: ZoomCópia, Let's copy (e.g., at Atrium Saldanha).

Meals / Conference dinner

All the lunches and coffee breaks, as well as the conference dinner, are included in the registration.

Lunches and coffee breaks will take place near the conference room Auditório 2.

The conference dinner will take place on Wednesday 13 July, at Centro Cultural de Belém (CCB), in the Belém neighbourhood of Lisbon. See here how to reach the venue.

Conference dinner programme:

  • 19:15 - Welcome drink and finger food appetisers
  • 20:00 - Dinner, including announcement of the winner of the International Quantum Award.

Information about COVID measures

For information regarding the arrival, please see the official pageCOVID-19 measures implemented in Portugal.

Portugal is one of the most vaccinated countries in the World.

Currently, masks are only required in public transportation, taxis and similar services, pharmacies, and hospitals and health facilities in general.

The conference room will be used at half capacity, so a distance can be kept between seats: please respect social distancing etiquette.
